Basic Sign Language Expressions

Planned for May 15, 2023 Communication and Language

Learning Objectives

  • Students will learn 10 basic ASL signs for everyday communication
  • Students will practice fingerspelling their names
  • Students will engage in simple signed conversations with peers

Materials Needed

  • ASL flashcards with common phrases
  • Mirrors for students to check their form
  • Video examples of ASL conversations
  • Name tags with fingerspelling guides

Activities

  1. Introduction to ASL and its importance (10 min)
  2. Demonstration and practice of 10 basic signs (15 min)
  3. Fingerspelling practice with name tags (10 min)
  4. Paired conversations using learned signs (10 min)
  5. Review and reflection (5 min)

Lesson Information

Duration: 50 minutes

Group Size: Small group (4 students)

IEP Goals Addressed:

  • COM.3.1: Use appropriate communication methods
  • SOC.2.2: Engage in reciprocal interactions
